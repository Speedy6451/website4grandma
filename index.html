Family Stories of Bipolar Disorder


Parents, partners, siblings, and adult children who have a relative living with bipolar disorder share how they pursued well-being for themselves and their loved ones. As you think about their experiences, you may be inspired and encouraged, find hope, reach acceptance, and learn to separate the uniqueness and essence of your family member from the mental illness.
Author Dr. Marjorie Schaffer, PhD, RN
